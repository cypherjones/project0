Playable Characters

There are 4 playable characters that come with the vanilla game and 2 more that come with the DLC content. Each character has three unique skill trees and abilities to choose from. As you build your skill tree you'll get boosts to your powers and extra abilites as well.

Axton (the Commando)

Axton is the playable commando class character. He is able to deploy a Dahl sabre turret, a mounted gun with 360° rotation that can be upgraded with many weapons and abilities. He has 3 skill trees.

  • Guerilla - This tree focuses on high damage attacks and has multiple upgrades for The Sabre Turret such as Laser sight and Sentry.
  • Gunpowder - This tree focuses on medium range explosive damage and weapon damage. It improves many of Axton's offensive combat stats, but improves the turret as a tactical deploy/distraction.
  • Survival - The third skill tree focuses mainly on defensive stats and using the turrent frequently. It features skills like Healthy, which gives +6% max health per level. Or Last Ditch Effort, which gives +8% gun damage and +14% movement speed per level during fight for your life.

Maya (The Siren)

Maya is the playable siren class. She has a phaselock ability that suspends her enemies in a different dimension. It can be upgraded to provide various damaging effects. Her three skill trees include.

  • Motion - This tree focuses on crowd control and defense. It has skills like Ward which gives +5% sheild capacity and -8% shield recharge delay per level.
  • Harmony - Harmony is Maya's second skill tree. It is mainly focused on healing herself and her teammates while also buffing her DPS stats.
  • Cataclysm - This tree mainly focuses on dealing elemental effects to her enemies. It feautures skills sick as Flicker which adds +6% elemental effect chance per level. This tree buffs damage but doesn't focus on healing.

Salvador (The Gunzerker)

Salvador is the Gunzereker class character in Borderlands 2. His main skill is Gunzerking, which allows his to dual weild any 2 weapons in the game for a limited time. His three skill trees are.

  • Gun Lust - This tree mainly focuses on swapping and reloading weapons and also gives salvador various gun abilities.
  • Rampage - This tree focuses on extra ammunition and Gunzekring. It includes skills such as Inconceivable, which gives a chance that shots fired do not consume ammo.
  • Brawn - This skill tree focuses on acting as a tank player. This tree allows you to soak up damage while losing outgoing damage.

Zer0 (The Assasin)

Zero is the playable assasin in Borderlands 2. His main skill is deception. It grant Zer0 the ability to deploy a holographis decoy of himself and go into a invisible stealth mode. He deals amplified damage on his next attack. His three skill threes are.

  • Sniping - The sniping tree focuses on, you guessed it, snipers. It heavily focuses on critical hits and ranged combat.
  • Cunning - This skill tree focuses on improving Deception and abilities that benifet ranged attacks as well as melee combat.
  • Bloodshed - This tree strongly focuses on melee combat alone. It includes skills such as killing blow, which increases melee damage against enemies with low health.
