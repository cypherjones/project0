The Story

The Borderlands 2 story is set 5 years after the events of Borderlands. Handsom Jack, the main antagonist, has taken over the hyperion corperation, declared himself dictator of Pandora, and taken all the credit for finding the vault. He even goes as far to say he killed the Destroyer (The endgame boss from Borderlands 1).

The story begins with the vault hunters on a train to begin their search for the vault. The train ride turned out to be a trap. Capitain Jack's goal is to get rid of anyone that's searching for the vault. The protagonists wake up in the middle of a frozen wasteland to CL4P-TP, a friendly steward bot, digging through the wreckage of the destroyed train.
